Hi, I'm Jeanika!

As a perfectionist, I treat every project I take on with the same care and dedication as if it were my own child. With a freshly earned Bachelor’s degree in Visual Communication, I’ve spent the past four years cultivating a deep love for design; especially in branding, editorial, and advertising. Whatever your project calls for, you can be sure I’ll pour my whole heart into it.

When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me carefully curating outfits, goofing around with my siblings, or exploring Calgary’s many coffee shops. So whether you’re looking to talk business or need a buddy to show you where to find the best croissants in town, I’m sure we can create something magical together.
