Panahon

branding || package design

2024

Panahon is a beer brand that transforms Filipino folklore into a multi-sensory experience—merging myth, flavour, and visual storytelling. Rooted in cultural pride and creative exploration, the brand celebrates the richness of Filipino heritage by bringing legendary creatures and tales to life through unique brews and expressive design.

Illustration plays a central role in the brand, with each label designed to capture the character’s spirit and the intensity of the flavor—ranging from serene to bold to magical. By blending traditional folklore with modern brewing, Noong Unang Panahon creates a new kind of narrative—one that’s meant to be sipped, shared, and remembered.
