Nine Tails Hot Sauce

branding || package design

2025

Inspired by legend, crafted for flavour.

Nine Tails is a hot sauce brand rooted in the mythology of the nine-tailed fox; an iconic figure across Asian folklore symbolising fire, cunning, and transformation. I leaned into storytelling as the heart of this project, using each flavour to represent a different side of the fox’s personality and heat level.

From a calm fox lounging for Mango Habanero, to a playful prance for Calamansi Chili, and a fierce battle between foxes for the intense Smoked Ghost Pepper. Each label illustration builds on the narrative of spice and spirit. The brand is designed for adventurous eaters who value bold flavours, rich stories, and striking design.
