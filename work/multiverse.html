How Shirley Kurata Dressed the Multiverse

experimental editorial design

2024

In this experimental editorial project, the focus was on evoking emotions rather than adhering strictly to legibility. Inspired by the captivating complexity of the film “Everything Everywhere All at Once,” I employed analog techniques to craft a layered editorial spread. Pull quotes were strategically overlaid on the article, creating a visual tapestry that mirrored the organized chaos of the movie’s narrative.

Through deliberate juxtaposition and blending of text and imagery, the project aimed to immerse the reader in the film’s intricate themes and sensations, inviting them to navigate through the depths of emotion and meaning. The result was a captivating exploration of visual storytelling, where the boundaries between comprehension and sensation blurred, echoing the transcendental experience of the cinematic masterpiece it sought to capture through its costumes and sets alike.
